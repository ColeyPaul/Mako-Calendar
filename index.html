Just Keep Calm and Calendar On...

It's 2025, and you shouldn't be spending your time manually adding every school event to your personal calendar. We've all been there—scrolling through endless capo pdfs, cross referencing the school website, looking at class messages to confirm a minimum day schedule... Wondering if it's "dress like a pirate" day, or getting a last-minute reminder to reload the book fair e-wallet. It's time to simplify.

We've created a new, unofficial Vista Del Mar 1st Grade Sharks calendar to help you get organized. This calendar is a combination of community submissions and AI-powered automation.

Here’s how it works: ►
  • It’s a combo of community and tech: We use AI agents and automation to pull information directly from various sources like Mako email messages, Room Parent emails, and teacher updates. This helps us cover the broad strokes of school life.
  • Your input matters: We also rely on manual input from our community. If you see something missing or have a new event to add, you can submit it to the calendar. This combination of AI and community coordination ensures the calendar is as accurate and complete as possible.
  • It syncs with your life: The best part? You can automatically sync this calendar with your personal one (Google Calendar, iCal, etc.). No more manual data entry or missed events. We're doing the heavy lifting so you can keep calm and calendar on.
